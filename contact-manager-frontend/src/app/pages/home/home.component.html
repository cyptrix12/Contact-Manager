Welcome to Contact Manager

Contact Manager is a simple tool to help you organize and manage your contacts efficiently.

You can add, edit, delete contacts, and keep track of important details like phone numbers, emails, and more.

Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Register an account (password must be at least 6 characters).
  2. Log in with your email and password.
  3. Go to the Contacts page and add a new contact.
  4. Edit a contact by clicking on it.
  5. Log out from the Login page.
  6. Check details without permission to edit.
